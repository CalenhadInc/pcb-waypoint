# USBLC6-2P6 Datasheet Summary

This document summarizes the key specifications for the USBLC6-2P6, a low-capacitance dual-line ESD protection device.

## Key Features

*   **Device:** USBLC6-2P6
*   **Manufacturer:** STMicroelectronics
*   **Type:** Low capacitance, dual line, bidirectional ESD protection
*   **Package:** SOT-666 (1.6 x 1.6 mm)

## Electrical Characteristics (at 25°C)

| Parameter | Symbol | Value | Unit | Condition |
| --- | --- | --- | --- | --- |
| Breakdown Voltage | VBR | 6.8 (min) | V | IR = 1 mA |
| Line Capacitance | CL | 1 (typ), 1.5 (max) | pF | VR = 0 V, F = 1 MHz |
| Leakage Current | IRM | 150 (max) | nA | VRM = 3 V |
| Leakage Current | IRM | 1 (max) | µA | VRM = 5.5 V |
| Clamping Voltage | VCL | 10 (typ) | V | IPP = 1 A (8/20 µs) |
| Clamping Voltage | VCL | 16 (typ) | V | IPP = 5 A (8/20 µs) |
| Peak Pulse Power | PPP | 80 | W | 8/20 µs |

## Protection and Compliance

*   **ESD Protection (Contact):** > 30 kV
*   **IEC 61000-4-2:** Level 4 compliant
*   **MIL-STD-883G Method 3015-7:** Class 3B (Human Body Model)

## Applications

*   High-speed data lines such as:
    *   USB 2.0
    *   Ethernet links
    *   Video lines
*   Suitable for tablets, notebooks, mobile phones, and datacom systems.

## Pin Configuration

*   **1, 6:** I/O 1
*   **2, 5:** I/O 2
*   **3, 4:** GND

## Ordering Information

*   **Order Code:** USBLC6-2P6
*   **Marking:** 62S
